Comprehensive Academic Guidance 

Our staff advisors assist you with long-term course planning, general education course information, university policy and procedures, and much more!

Scroll down to contact the staff advisor for your program.

Faculty advisors help you in areas such as career planning, future research and senior project opportunities. Your faculty advisor information can be found in your department's office or homepage.

We are located in 17-2257. We have a friendly staff available to assist you M-F 8 AM - 5 PM. We offer a variety of appointments during these hours, including some drop in times. See below for more information!

Make an Appointment or do a Drop In

Appointment Location and Hours: Virtual or In Person (17-2257) | Monday-Thursday 9:30a-11:30a and 1:30p-3:30p

There are a variety of virtual and in person appointments available. To make an appointment with your advisor, click on your advisor's name below, or use CPP Connect.

Drop-In Location and Hours: Virtual or In Person (17-2257) | Monday-Thursday 8:30a-9:30a, 3:30p-4:30p and Friday 9a-11a

Drop in advising is intended to go over quick questions or help you with inquiries regarding change of major. If you need to discuss long term course planning or have several questions, you are encouraged to make an appointment.

 

To access drop in hours virtually, you may use the DROP IN HOURS link below for your assigned advisor.
