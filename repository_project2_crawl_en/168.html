Urban and Regional Planning Chapter

President: Manuel Munoz ’09

The Urban and Regional Planning Alumni Chapter is dedicated to strengthening the connections between URP alumni and the entire URP community. Chartered in 1994, the chapter was formed to assist alumni in furthering their personal and professional, growth and development.

The department seeks the ongoing involvement of alumni. There are many opportunities to get involved, from the Professor for a Day program, to mentoring students, to advising us on ways to improve our curriculum. The Urban and Regional Planning Alumni Chapter is certified and active.
