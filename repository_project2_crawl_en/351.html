College of Engineering Scholarships

Scholarship Opportunities from $500 to $10,000. Apply Today!

This page lists up-to-date information on CPP Engineering scholarships. Scroll to review opportunities, award amounts and application information.

How to Apply for a Scholarship

  1. Fill out the general Bronco Scholarship Application. This application lets you apply for multiple scholarships on the portal, unless...
  2. ... the scholarship has specific requirements, like short statements. In that case, fulfill additional requirements.

For questions on how scholarships affect your existing financial aid, contact scholarships@cpp.edu.

For previous scholarship recipients, visit this page.
