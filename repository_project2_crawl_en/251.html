Outstanding Alumni

The Distinguished Alumni Awards is the highest honor presented by the Cal Poly Alumni Association. Selected through a highly competitive process, each recipient has made a lasting impact on society and Cal Poly Pomona through outstanding professional, philanthropic, or volunteer accomplishments.

  • Learn more about the Distinguished Alumni Awards criteria and nomination process. 
  • Each year, the Cal Poly Pomona community gathers to celebrate the achievements of our alumni community at an Alumni Awards Gala. 
  • Learn more about the Distinguished Alumni of Cal Poly Pomona!

At Cal Poly Pomona, we are always proud of the amazing work of our alumni. Our Alumni Spotlight series highlights the work of individuals who are using their talents to make a difference in the world.
