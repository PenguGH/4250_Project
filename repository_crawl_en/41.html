    Our Philosophy

    From Seeds to Food and Fashion

    Agriculture touches all of our lives, from the food we eat to the clothing we wear. At the Huntley College of Agriculture, you will receive an education that prepares you to work on some part of that vast chain, whether it’s growing plants or designing clothes in a more sustainable manner, raising and caring for animals, educating people about eating healthier, creating new food products, or marketing and promoting these goods.

    Real-World Growing

    Experience traditional, organic and urban farming.

    Working with Animals

    Learn about raising and caring for animals large and small.

    Business Skills

    Agriculture, fashion and food are big business. Discover opportunities in marketing, finance and the supply chain.

    Creating Food

    There's an art and science to making new food products.

    Fashion Lines

    Learn how to create a fashion line, from design to sourcing and production.

    Helping Others

    Help people live healthier lives through proper nutrition.

    College Transitions

    Academic Programs

    View All Programs
    Bachelor's

    Agribusiness & Food Industry Management

    Combine your passion for growing plants or raising animals with the skills you need to run your own business, a startup, or a global conglomerate.
    Bachelor's

    Agricultural Science

    Get a strong foundation in agricultural basics that will give you the versatility to pursue a wide variety of careers in the industry. Combine that with specialization in one of our many minors.

    Bachelor's

    Animal Health Science

    With this degree, you are eligible to take the Veterinary Technician National exam and become a registered veterinary technologist, the equivalent of a nurse.
    Bachelor's

    Animal Science

    Want to become a veterinarian? Study animal science. But this major also offers other options working as a researcher, farm manager, or in the animal health or food production industries.
    Bachelor's

    Apparel Merchandising & Management

    Get a comprehensive education preparing you to work in the global apparel industry, whether it's in design and production, fashion retailing, or textiles.

    Bachelor's

    Food Science & Technology

    Employ science, statistics, engineering, and technology to create healthier foods that taste better, are easier to produce, and last longer.
    Bachelor's

    Nutrition

    Help people live healthier lives by becoming registered dietitians, health coaches or researchers. You can work in healthcare, food service management or community nutrition settings.
    Bachelor's

    Plant Science

    Learn the science behind farming and growing plants. Graduates have found work as farm managers, viticulturists, sports turf managers, pest control advisors, urban farmers, and arborists.

    Master's

    Agriculture - Agricultural Science Option

    Gain the skills and the knowledge to address challenges and critical issues that affect agriculture in rural and urban areas and become an effective advocate for the areas and issues you are most passionate about.

    Master's

    Agriculture - Animal Science Option

    Develop research skills and prepare yourself to work in animal production, business, or animal-related industries. This program also will help you gain entrance to professional schools or prepare you to pursue a doctorate.
    Master's

    Agriculture - Nutrition & Food Science Option

    With this degree, you can prepare yourself to work in the food industry or clinical settings, teach, conduct research, or make progress toward earning a doctorate.
    Master's

    Agriculture - Plant Science Option

    In this program, you'll complete a thesis research project in the field, a greenhouse or a lab. Your experience will prepare you to work in research in commercial, academic, or government settings or to pursue a doctorate.
    Master's

    Dietetics

    This online degree program will prepare you for careers as a registered dietitian or a registered dietitian nutritionist or a dietetic internship.
    Post-Baccalaureate

    Dietetic Internship

    Want to help people live better lives through nutrition? Complete the required supervised practice to become a registered dietitian through the dietetic internship program.
