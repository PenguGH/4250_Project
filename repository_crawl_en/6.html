    • Become Society's Problem Solver

    See the world as an engineer and invent solutions that make everyday life better.

    Problem Solved...

    ... now how can it be better?

    The difference between wishful thinking and real-world problem solving is all in the doing. At the College of Engineering, you'll learn to think beyond what’s already been done to make it better than ever. You'll put theory to work through hands-on exposure, real-world application and cutting-edge research — preparing you to enter an array of industries with utmost experience, confidence and problem-solving abilities. At Cal Poly Pomona, you don’t just learn by doing. You become by doing.
