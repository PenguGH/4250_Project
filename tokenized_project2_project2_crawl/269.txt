Ambassadors Honor Cord Diploma Information Honor Cord A gold cord worn around the neck of the gown signifies baccalaureate graduation with academic honors Cal Poly Pomona grants honors upon graduation to students who have demonstrated academic excellence throughout their undergraduate careers Graduates receiving master s postbaccalaureate degrees are not eligible for honors Honors apply to undergraduate students with an overall grade point average of at least 3 50 at the beginning of Spring term For additional information Everyone who earns honors at graduation will have that designation noted on their diploma The honors are Summa Cum Laude 3 80 4 00 Magna Cum Laude 3 65 3 79 Cum Laude 3 50 3 64 Next distribution will begin on April 7 through May 8 at the Bronco Advising Center located on the first floor of Student Service Building SSB 121 East For more information please email commencement cpp edu Diploma Information Diplomas are sent to the students mailing addresses on file Those to whom a degree has been awarded will receive a diploma email from the Registrar s office asking that they verify the accuracy of their mailing address This notice will be sent to the Cal Poly Pomona email account The Senior Exit Survey is attached to the diploma email The diploma will be mailed out once the survey is submitted To change your address on line please
