February 11 2025 How can the food and agriculture industries use artificial intelligence What are the opportunities and challenges That s the focus of the 2025 Winter Food Agri Tech Forum which is a collaboration between the Huntley College of Agriculture and the Swiss American Chamber of Commerce s Southern California Chapter The event will be held from 9 to 11 a m on Thursday Feb 27 at AGRIscapes and live streamed with participants from Europe The discussion will be led by an international panel comprising senior executives from technology providers and academics from engineering computer science food and agriculture backgrounds Discussion topics include An overview of Artificial Intelligence technologies and their diffusion Early AI applications and latent opportunities for AI in food and agriculture Examples of successful applications of AI in food and agriculture How AI impacts business processes supply chain relationships and industry structure The emerging support infrastructure in food and agriculture toward AI adoption Strategies to explore AI and overcome financial and technical barriers to adoption Implications for small and medium sized companies Ethical social regulatory and security issues For registration and more information visit https www cpp edu agri food agri tech forum shtml Apply Maps Visit Contact Us Copyright 2025 California State
