Renaissance Scholars Undocumented Students Services Undocumented Students Services About Us Since its inception in 2017 Undocumented Students Services USS has served as a brave space where students can build relationships with faculty and staff that support their academic success build community and be connected with on and off campus resources throughout their educational journey We provide virtual and in person services to accommodate the needs of students USS professional staff are located at the Bronco Dreamers Resource Center BDRC in building 26 room 101 Through our programs and initiatives we foster a sense of belonging create opportunities for personal and professional growth and empower students to thrive in an inclusive and supportive environment Cal Poly Pomona is a
