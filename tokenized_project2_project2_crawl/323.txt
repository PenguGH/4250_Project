for her leadership roles at SunDensity Inc as CEO and board member where she drove scale up operations and raised capital for photonics smart coatings With over 28 years of industry expertise spanning power generation energy semiconductor and big data she previously held strategic positions at General Electric focusing on industrial automation Industrial Internet of Things IIoT and new business
