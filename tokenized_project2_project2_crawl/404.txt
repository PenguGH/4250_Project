Horseshoe Award is a new way to recognize Student Affairs colleagues who go above and beyond Anyone can send a Horseshoe award to a DSA staff member throughout the year as an easy way to say thank you The recipient will be sent a printable award via email with a message of gratitude from their nominator All Horseshoe award winners each academic year will be recognized at the DSA End of Year
