Management Now accepting applications for Fall 2025 admission Program Overview The Master of Science in Hospitality Management MSHM degree exposes students to advanced management concepts including accounting and finance marketing strategic management and human resources management as well as qualitative and quantitative analytical skills as they pertain specifically to the hospitality industry The program is designed to prepare graduates with the depth of hospitality knowledge and skills that will equip them to make an immediate impact in the highly competitive hospitality industry or to provide the foundation for the pursuit of a terminal degree at an academic institution The MSHM program is sufficiently flexible to recognize the full range of skills and knowledge needed for
