Philosophy A True Polytechnic Experience The College of Environmental Design ENV connects people places and the environments in which we live work and play A leader in design excellence our programs use the rich creative economy and cultural capital of Southern California and its geographical advantage as a global gateway for trade and ideas to educate and train future designers to shape the future of the built and natural environments with resiliency inclusivity and justice ENV offers undergraduate and graduate degrees in architecture landscape architecture urban and regional planning
