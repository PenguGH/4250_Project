AACSB Accreditation The undergraduate programs of the College of Business Administration and the graduate degrees offered by the Singelyn Graduate School of Business are accredited by the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business AACSB All our degree programs meet the rigorous requirements of AACSB accreditation focusing on the principles of continuous improvement demonstrated student learning societal impact and mission aligned curricula AACSB accreditation is globally recognized as the longest standing and most recognized form of business school accreditation Accreditation is a voluntary nongovernmental process that includes a rigorous external review of a school s mission faculty qualifications curricula and ability to provide the highest quality programs while making a societal impact AACSB is recognized by the world s top employers allowing our students access to broad career opportunities AACSB accreditation is achieved by only 6 of business schools worldwide Recent surveys demonstrate that graduates of AACSB accredited business schools have high employability degree satisfaction and career advancement rates This accreditation allows our students to be part of the prestigious Beta Gamma Sigma honor society and in general places us among the best education programs in the world Did you know Rigorous Standards Less than 6 percent of business schools
