Prof Nissenson Wins ASEE National Best Teacher Award ME Students Win 3rd Place in ASHRAE International Competition Previous Next About the Mechanical Engineering Program The Mechanical Engineering program provides a quality well rounded education that is based on imparting fundamental knowledge and skills in mathematics and pure science as well as engineering science and design Mechanical Engineering graduates are uniquely suited to jobs in all traditional areas of mechanical engineering including mechanics machine design energy systems fluid flow heat transfer and thermodynamics To ensure this all students take the same fundamental courses but then choose a series of technical electives geared towards their own interests or career objectives Additional experience is gained through student design projects and voluntary participation in student clubs honor societies and design competitions The Mechanical Engineering Department offers a Mechanical Engineering program leading to a Bachelor of Science degree The Mechanical Engineering program is accredited by the Engineering Accreditation Commission of ABET https www abet org under the General Criteria and the Program Criteria for Mechanical and Similarly Named Engineering Programs The Mechanical Engineering Department also offers a graduate program leading to a Master of Science degree Apply Maps Visit
