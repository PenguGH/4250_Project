Students Students looking for job opportunities should check Handshake the primary career services platform at Cal Poly Pomona Check Handshake Many of our learn by doing majors hire students to assist technicians in laboratories faculty members with teaching and or research activities and department staff with department office business Students can inquire in their major s departmental office to see if their program hires student assistants and what types they hire AGRIscapes also hires students to work seasonally for the annual Pumpkin Fest and fall and spring field trips Jobs are posted on the Cal Poly Pomona Enterprises also known as Cal Poly Pomona Foundation Inc website Employment Opportunites at Cal Poly Pomona Enterprises The Associated Students Inc ASI also employs students Employment Opportunites at ASI In addition many administrative offices on campus hire students in a variety of capacities Students have succeeded in balancing work on campus with their classes and found academic success All these positions are advertised through the Cal Poly Pomona Career Center but can only be seen by current students The Career Center also has numerous resources for students
