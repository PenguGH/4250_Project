Student Parent Priority Registration

Student parents have priority registration under California law (AB 2881). Student parent is defined as a 'student who has a child or children under 18 years of age who receive more than half of their support from that student.' 

Verification Instructions

  • You must submit an application using the form linked below. You will need to sign in using your Bronco name and password.
  • Provide the birthdate of your youngest dependent.
  • Upload approved documents.
  • You will receive a confirmation email.

Priority registration allows students to register for their next academic term's classes earlier than general registration. It is granted to student groups approved by the Academic Senate each term.

Other student groups receive priority registration based on mandated policy governed by federal and state regulations (e.g., military veterans, disability, foster youth, etc.)
