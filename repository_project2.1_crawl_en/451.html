    Mission & Vision

    • Office of Academic Innovation

    The Offices of Academic Innovation curate experiential learning, discovery and innovation opportunities for the Cal Poly Pomona community.

    The Office of Academic Innovation (OAI) is dedicated to fostering student success by cultivating a culture of creativity and innovation in teaching and learning. Aligned with Cal Poly Pomona’s mission to promote success through experiential learning, discovery, and innovation, OAI supports the university’s strategic goal of becoming the epicenter of creativity, innovation, and discovery. Through collaborative efforts with faculty, staff, students, alumni, and external partners we design and implement innovative curricula that inspire engaged, lifelong learning across the Cal Poly Pomona community.

    Faculty and Staff

    Explore Opportunities to Transform Higher Education

    The Office of Academic Innovation (OAI) believes in inspiring faculty and staff to lead the charge in redefining education. Through innovative resources and collaborative opportunities, we foster an environment where creativity flourishes, professional growth thrives, and transformative learning takes shape for the Cal Poly Pomona community.

    Innovate the Future of Academia at Summer Conferences

    Participate in professional development sessions to enhance teaching practices, explore innovative pedagogy, and develop curriculum design skills with guidance from national experts.

    Support Experiential Learning by making your class a PolyX

    PolyX at Cal Poly Pomona enables students to tackle field challenges and create innovative solutions. Through collaboration and mentorship, faculty and staff prepare students for success beyond the classroom.

    Develop Innovative curriculum at Winter Institutes

    Participate in professional development sessions to enhance teaching practices, explore innovative pedagogy, and develop curriculum design skills with guidance from national experts.

    Mentor Student Micro-Interns

    Guide students in short-term, project-based internships that align with your expertise and organizational goals, fostering professional growth for both mentors and students.

    Engage with Digital Credentialing

    Incorporate digital badges to recognize and validate specific skills, accomplishments, and learning outcomes for students, colleagues, or teams.

    Volunteer at Bronco Stampede of Service

    Join university-wide service events that unite the CPP community in supporting local organizations and causes.

    Create Innovative Pop-Up Courses

    Design and teach unique courses that address emerging topics and provide students with dynamic learning experiences.

    Teach in the Honors Community

    Lead specialized courses and mentor high-achieving students in their academic journey.

    Incorporate Service Learning

    Integrate community service into academic coursework for real-world impact.

    Alumni Making an Impact

    Alumni can engage and inspire the Cal Poly Pomona community. By seizing transformative opportunities to mentor, collaborate, and support student success, alumni play a vital role in shaping the next generation of leaders while making a positive impact on their alma mater and beyond.

    Industry and Community Partners

    Get the PolyX Advantage

    Discover how your organization can contribute to a vibrant future while gaining access to emerging talent and impactful collaboration opportunities.

    Engage in Workforce Development Initiatives

    Partner with The Hub to invigorate Pomonas workforce through comprehensive programs in science, technology, and entrepreneurship. This collaboration aims to empower the local community and foster economic growth.

    Provide Short-Term, Project-Based Internships

    Organizations can offer micro-internships to Cal Poly Pomona students, allowing them to gain practical experience while contributing to your projects. This engagement supports student career readiness and allows partners to access emerging talent.

    Get Access to Skilled Talent

    Partner with the Career Center and recruit students to learn more about open jobs and internship opportunities.

    Support Service Learning and Cooperative Education

    Engage with the university by integrating community service with academic coursework, offering practical experiences that benefit both students and the community. This involvement promotes civic responsibility and experiential learning.
