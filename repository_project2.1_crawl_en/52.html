Bronco Community Invited to Review Draft EIR Related to Campus Master Plan

  • University News

May 05, 2025 | melanie johnson

Share

The Cal Poly Pomona campus community is invited to review and comment on the draft Environmental Impact Report (EIR) for the Campus Master Plan update.

The EIR is a technical document informing the public about potential impacts projects may have on the physical environment. The draft EIR review and comment period will run from May 7 to June 20. Draft EIR documents and instructions for commenting may be accessed on the Environmental Impact Report page of the 2040 Campus Master Plan Update website.  

A Public Information Meeting will be held virtually on May 13, from 6 to 7 p.m. Visit the upcoming events page on the 2040 Campus Master Plan Update website or the Zoom Webinar Link for more information.
