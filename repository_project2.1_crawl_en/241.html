About the Option

The Marketing Management option is designed to give students an understanding of the factors both within the internal and external environments that affect the development and implementation of plans to serve markets and to attain economic goals. Emphasis is placed on determining market needs and decision-making concerning product, pricing, promotion and distribution strategies required to meet those market needs.

Career Outcomes

Account Manager

Overseeing marketing campaigns, and ensure that projects align with the client’s goals and expectations

Advertising Director

Create, implement and manage the success of advertising campaigns

Brand Planning Coordinator

Assist in the development, coordination, and execution of brand strategies to ensure consistency and effectiveness across various marketing channels

Marketing Coordinator

Support marketing efforts, organize campaigns, and ensure that marketing projects are executed effectively

Media Planner

Conduct market research to understand target audiences, including their demographics, behavior, and media consumption habits

Public Relations Executive

Manage an organization’s image and reputation, build relationships with the public, media, and stakeholders, and craft communication strategies to promote positive publicity

Research Analyst

Collect, analyze, and interpret data to help organizations understand market trends, customer behavior, and the competitive landscape

Sales Manager

Oversee both marketing and sales activities to ensure that the organization effectively promotes its products or services and achieves revenue goals

Social Media Strategist

Develop and implement social media strategies to enhance a brand’s online presence, engage its target audience, and drive business goals.

Overview

Through proper selection of courses, with advisor approval, each student will develop and complete an individualized program of courses that will prepare him or her for a specialized career field within the field of marketing management. The specialized fields from which the student will choose are: (1) promotion, (2) professional selling, (3) entertainment, (4) marketing research, and (5) general marketing.
