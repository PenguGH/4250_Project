Board of Directors

The Cal Poly Pomona Philanthropic Foundation Board of Directors  is made up of alumni and friends who are committed to the fundraising success and long-term fiscal health of the university. Members serve as ambassadors to raise the university’s profile in the region and help the university engage with donors and corporate partners.
