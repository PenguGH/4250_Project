Evacuation Chairs

Evacuation Chairs (Evac Chairs) have been purchased by the Office of Emergency Management and will soon be deployed in each multi-story building on campus. Evac Chairs are designed as a universal solution to assist building occupants in descending a stairway in an emergency. These light-weight chars are easy to use, require minimal training and effortlessly glide down stairs.
